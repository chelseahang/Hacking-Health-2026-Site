    James Guy

    Country Lead, Canada & General Manager, Specialty Care — Sanofi

    James Guy leads Sanofi Canada and oversees operations across three business units. With over two decades of pharmaceutical industry experience and 13 years at Sanofi in global, US, and Canadian leadership roles, he brings strategic expertise across immunology, respiratory, and specialty care markets.

    Dr. Dante Morra

    Founder and Chair — CAN Health Network

    Dr. Dante Morra is a visionary leader transforming Canadian healthcare through innovation and strategic system change. As Founder and Chair of the CAN Health Network, he has revolutionized how medtech companies succeed in Canada's healthcare sector. Previously, he served as Chief Clinical Innovation Officer at THP Solutions, founded Insight Health Solutions ($45M annual initiative), and as inaugural Chief of Staff at Trillium Health Partners, where he led Canada's largest hospital merger, overseeing 1,400 physicians. His expertise spans healthcare innovation, system transformation, and operational excellence.
