Code of Conduct

Facility Guidelines

Please do not take snacks and beverages from the Lodge/Kitchen area, those are for Snowflake employees only. Refreshments will be provided at designated times.

Professional Behavior

Maintain professional conduct throughout this official Sanofi event. You are among leadership and experts across the organization. Dress professionally and be respectful to all attendees.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code?

Please come dressed in business casual attire.

What should I bring?

Laptop and charger.

Where is the hackathon taking place?

The Snowflake office, floors 31 and 32.

Will food be provided during the event?

Yes, breakfast and lunch on both days.

What if I have questions during the event?

If it's admin related, ask in the Teams channel. If it's related to your problem space, ask your data expert.

How do I submit my project?

Please submit your code to your team's final submission folder by 1:00 PM on Day 2.

What happens to winning projects?

Winning projects will go through the 10 day governance process to implement the tool into the designated product.
