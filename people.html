    Country Lead, Canada & General Manager, Specialty Care — Sanofi

    Dr. Dante Morra

    Founder and Chair — CAN Health Network

    Dr. Dante Morra is a visionary leader transforming Canadian healthcare through innovation and strategic system change. As Founder and Chair of the CAN Health Network, he has revolutionized how medtech companies succeed in Canada's healthcare sector. He previously served as inaugural Chief of Staff at Trillium Health Partners, where he led Canada's largest hospital merger overseeing 1,400 physicians, and founded Insight Health Solutions, a $45M annual initiative. His expertise spans healthcare innovation, system transformation, and operational excellence.
